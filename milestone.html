OUR MILESTONE

Year 2021

Varuna Electric

After 85 years of excellence, the company decided to rebrand itself from Varuna Pumps to Varuna Electric. We launched our new logo and tagline,‘Har Boond mein Josh’

Year 2021

Crossed 500 Cro0e Mark

Varuna Electric is one of the fastest growing companies in the Pump Industry. Amid the tensions and fear of Covid-19, the hustle and bustle of Varuna Team, its Channel Partners and confidence of its customers helped us to achieve the magic number of Rs. 500 Cr. in one financial year. We celebrated the day with everyone associated with Varuna by having a cake cutting ceremony at every stakeholder’s place.

Year 2018

Crossed 100 Crore Mark

We believe in celebrating every little achievement and this was a huge year for everyone who believes and tirelessly works for the brand that is aimed to grow and improve everyday. Within 4 years of launch, for the first time we crossed the Rs. 100 crore mark in International Business.

Year 2017

Joining Hand For A Better Tommorow

We believe in celebrating every little achievement and this was a huge year for everyone who believes and tirelessly works for the brand that is aimed to grow and improve everyday. Within 4 years of launch, for the first time we crossed the Rs. 100 crore mark in International Business.

Year 2016

Brand Initative

Following the immense success of Radio Campaign, Varuna Electric decided to take a step further in the brand awareness program. For this, we launched 2 TVC advertising campaigns which were broadcasted on National Channels in India.

Year 2015

Brand Initative

To make bigger impacts in the market, Varuna Electric decided to create brand awareness with the objective to reach its end customers. For this, the key was Network development – build confidence in the network in terms of marketing support, service support and product quality. Launched a radio campaign followed by aggressive marketing.

Year 2014

Local For Global

Backed by a strong domestic presence and 16 branches nationwide, Varuna Electric is now exporting globally.

Year 2013

EEPC Award

For the 2nd Consecutive year, Varuna Electric was recognized by EEPC. Mr. Varun Gajjar attended the program held in Mumbai.

Year 2012

EEPC

Engineering Export Promotion Council of India recognized the team of Varuna for their contribution in the EEPC India 44th National Awards for Export Excellence 2011-12. Mr. Varun Gajjar attended the event and shared the stage with the dignitaries.

Year 1997

Emerging Market Leaders

Being the agricultural economy, the focus on the sector and continuous improvement in the product line helped us gain immense momentum in the market. Varuna Oil-filled Motor became the forerunner in the 100 mm Submersible Pump market and has been leading the way for the industry since then.

Year 1992

Intorducing The Firts Oil Field Submersible

The innovation and development of the product line has been the key for our success since inception. What began with a Hand Blower in 1935 has now conceptualised the use of Oil-filled Submersible Motors for the 100 mm sector and introduction of first Oil-filled Submersible Motor in India.

Year 1981

Expansion Of Product Line

The new enterprise, La-Gajjar Machineries brought Bore-well Submersible and Open-well Submersible, keeping the quality of the products intact.

Year 1950

Largest Manufacturer Of India

The milestone of 1950 was laid on the principle that the company envisions the long term vision and is proof that we have taken steps in the right direction since the beginning. One of the largest manufacturers in India with expertise in Hand Blowers, Power Blowers, Couple, Monoblock Centrifugal Pumps and Electric Motors

Year 1935

The Beginning

A unique product – hand blower introduced to India by a visionary industrialist late Shri Laljibhai J. Gajjar. A small step that turned the face of the Pump Industry in the decades to follow.
